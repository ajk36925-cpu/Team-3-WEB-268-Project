About Us

Rolls Express was founded five years ago in Pennsylvania with one signature idea at the heart of everything we do: fresh, flavorful spring rolls made with quality ingredients and bold, modern flavors. What began as a small local concept has grown into a fast‑moving brand that’s redefining what a quick, delicious meal can be. 

Our team has expanded to more than 30 passionate employees who bring energy, creativity, and dedication to every roll we serve. From the kitchen to customer service, each team member plays a key role in delivering the experience our guests love—fast, friendly, and always fresh. 

As demand for our signature spring rolls continues to grow, so does our company. We’re now expanding across the region and steadily building our presence throughout the United States. Even as we grow, our mission stays the same: serve exceptional food, create memorable experiences, and build a team that reflects the heart of the communities we serve. Rolls Express isn’t just a restaurant—it’s a team, a vision, and a movement built around one simple promise: great spring rolls, made with care, served with pride.
