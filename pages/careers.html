Roll with Us

Kitchen Roll Specialist

Prepares fresh rolls, bowls, and appetizers with precision and consistency while maintaining high food quality standards. Responsible for ingredient prep, assembly, and ensuring all dishes meet presentation and taste expectations. Works closely with the kitchen team to keep service fast and efficient.

Front Counter Team Member

Greets customers, takes orders, and provides a friendly, welcoming experience from start to finish. Handles payments, answers menu questions, and ensures orders are accurate and delivered promptly. Plays a key role in creating a positive and energetic atmosphere.

Catering & Events Coordinator

Manages catering orders from inquiry to delivery, helping customers select menu options and ensuring all event details are organized. Coordinates with the kitchen and staff to prepare large orders and ensures timely, accurate delivery or pickup. Focuses on delivering a smooth and professional catering experience.

Shift Lead / Assistant Manager

Oversees daily operations during assigned shifts, ensuring staff performance, customer satisfaction, and food quality standards are met. Assists with scheduling, training, and problem-solving while maintaining a clean and efficient work environment. Acts as the main point of contact for both staff and customers during service.
